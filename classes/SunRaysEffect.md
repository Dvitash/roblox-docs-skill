# SunRaysEffect

**Superclass:** [PostEffect](PostEffect.md)

Renders dynamic rays from the sun, affecting visibility based on intensity and spread.

## Properties
### `SunRaysEffect.Intensity`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the opacity of the sun rays.

### `SunRaysEffect.Spread`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines how wide the sun rays will spread out.
