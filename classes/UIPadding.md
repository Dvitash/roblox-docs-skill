# UIPadding

**Superclass:** [UIComponent](UIComponent.md)

Applies padding to the borders of a parent `Class.GuiObject` based on a specified amount or percentage of its size.

## Properties
### `UIPadding.PaddingBottom`
- **Type:** `UDim`
- **Summary:** Padding to apply on the bottom side, relative to the parent's normal size.

### `UIPadding.PaddingLeft`
- **Type:** `UDim`
- **Summary:** Padding to apply on the left side, relative to the parent's normal size.

### `UIPadding.PaddingRight`
- **Type:** `UDim`
- **Summary:** Padding to apply on the right side, relative to the parent's normal size.

### `UIPadding.PaddingTop`
- **Type:** `UDim`
- **Summary:** Padding to apply on the top side, relative to the parent's normal size.
