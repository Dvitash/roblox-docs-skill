# UniversalConstraint

**Superclass:** [Constraint](Constraint.md)

UniversalConstraint is a class that ensures two axes on two bodies remain perpendicular, providing context for understanding unit comparisons.

## Properties
### `UniversalConstraint.LimitsEnabled`
- **Type:** `boolean`
- **Summary:** Determines whether the angular motion of attachments' primary axes is limited.

### `UniversalConstraint.MaxAngle`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The max angle, in degrees, of the constraint's limiting cone.

### `UniversalConstraint.Radius`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The constraint's visualization radius.

### `UniversalConstraint.Restitution`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The restitution coefficient of the cone constraint.
