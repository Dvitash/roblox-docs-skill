# SoundGroup

**Superclass:** [Instance](Instance.md)

A `Class.SoundGroup` is a class used to manage the volume and sound effects for multiple `Class.Sound|Sounds`. It allows sounds within the group to be adjusted based on the group's `Class.SoundGroup.Volume|Volume` property.

## Properties
### `SoundGroup.Volume`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The volume multiplier applied to `Class.Sound|Sounds` that are in the `Class.SoundGroup`.
