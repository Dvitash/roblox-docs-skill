# Torque

**Superclass:** [Constraint](Constraint.md)

The `Torque` constraint applies constant torque to an assembly from its center of mass, affecting the resulting velocity.

## Properties
### `Torque.RelativeTo`
- **Type:** `ActuatorRelativeTo`
- **Summary:** The `Datatype.CFrame` in which the torque is expressed.

### `Torque.Torque`
- **Type:** `Vector3`
- **Summary:** The strength and direction of the torque.
