# SurfaceLight

**Superclass:** [Light](Light.md)

A `SurfaceLight` is a light source that emits illumination based on a specified color and brightness from a specific face, inheriting from `Class.BasePart` or `Class.Attachment`.

## Properties
### `SurfaceLight.Angle`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The angle of which the light is shone from the SurfaceLight.

### `SurfaceLight.Face`
- **Type:** `NormalId`
- **Summary:** Sets the side of the parent that the SurfaceLight comes from.

### `SurfaceLight.Range`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The distance from the SurfaceLight's face that will illuminate.
