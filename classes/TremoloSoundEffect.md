# TremoloSoundEffect

**Superclass:** [SoundEffect](SoundEffect.md)

TremoloSoundEffect is a class for creating a trembling effect on a sound by varying its volume up and down. It can be applied to `Class.Sound` or `Class.SoundGroup`.

## Properties
### `TremoloSoundEffect.Depth`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Controls how much the volume will raise and lower.

### `TremoloSoundEffect.Duty`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Controls how long during one volume oscillation the effect will be active.

### `TremoloSoundEffect.Frequency`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Sets how often the effect will oscillate the volume.
