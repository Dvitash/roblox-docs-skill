# Texture

**Superclass:** [Decal](Decal.md)

Applies a repeating image texture to the face of a parent `Class.BasePart` based on the `Class.Texture` property.

## Properties
### `Texture.OffsetStudsU`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the offset in studs of the rendered texture's horizontal coordinate.

### `Texture.OffsetStudsV`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the offset in studs of the rendered texture's vertical coordinate.

### `Texture.StudsPerTileU`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Sets the horizontal size, in studs, of the tiled image applied by the `Class.Texture`.

### `Texture.StudsPerTileV`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Sets the vertical size, in studs, of the tiled image applied by the `Class.Texture`.
