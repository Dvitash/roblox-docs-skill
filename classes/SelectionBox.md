# SelectionBox

**Superclass:** [InstanceAdornment](InstanceAdornment.md)

Renders a 3D box around `Class.PVAdornment.Adornee` when it is a descendant of `Class.Workspace` or `Class.GuiBase3d`.

## Properties
### `SelectionBox.LineThickness`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the thickness of the boxes outlines, in studs.

### `SelectionBox.SurfaceColor`
- **Type:** `BrickColor`
- **Tags:** Hidden, NotReplicated, Deprecated
- **Summary:** A `Datatype.BrickColor` version of `Class.SelectionBox.SurfaceColor3|SurfaceColor3`.

### `SelectionBox.SurfaceColor3`
- **Type:** `Color3`
- **Summary:** Determines the color of the box's surfaces.

### `SelectionBox.SurfaceTransparency`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the transparency of the box's surfaces.
