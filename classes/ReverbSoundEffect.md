# ReverbSoundEffect

**Superclass:** [SoundEffect](SoundEffect.md)

ReverbSoundEffect is a class for simulating the bouncing of audio sounds off surfaces, affecting echoes and volume. It controls the number of reflections generated and the smoothness of the simulated surfaces.

## Properties
### `ReverbSoundEffect.DecayTime`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Sets how long it takes for the reverberating echoes to fade out completely.

### `ReverbSoundEffect.Density`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Controls how many reflections are generated.

### `ReverbSoundEffect.Diffusion`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Controls how smooth and reflective the simulated surfaces are.

### `ReverbSoundEffect.DryLevel`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of the original sound.

### `ReverbSoundEffect.WetLevel`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of the echoed effect.
