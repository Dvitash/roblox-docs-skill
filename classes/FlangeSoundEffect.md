# FlangeSoundEffect

**Superclass:** [SoundEffect](SoundEffect.md)

The FlangeSoundEffect class creates a sweeping or swooshing effect on a sound by copying the original audio signal and modulating it slightly.

## Properties
### `FlangeSoundEffect.Depth`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The intensity of the effect.

### `FlangeSoundEffect.Mix`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Percentage of the original sound that will be applied to the filter.

### `FlangeSoundEffect.Rate`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The frequency that the effect oscillates at.
