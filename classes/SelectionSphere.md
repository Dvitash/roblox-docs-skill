# SelectionSphere

**Superclass:** [PVAdornment](PVAdornment.md)

Renders a 3D sphere around `Class.PVAdornment.Adornee` when it is a descendant of `Class.Workspace` or `Class.GuiBase3d`.

## Properties
### `SelectionSphere.SurfaceColor`
- **Type:** `BrickColor`
- **Tags:** Hidden, NotReplicated, Deprecated
- **Summary:** A `Datatype.BrickColor` version of `Class.SelectionBox.SurfaceColor3|SurfaceColor3`.

### `SelectionSphere.SurfaceColor3`
- **Type:** `Color3`
- **Summary:** Determines the color of the sphere's surface.

### `SelectionSphere.SurfaceTransparency`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the transparency of the sphere's surface.
