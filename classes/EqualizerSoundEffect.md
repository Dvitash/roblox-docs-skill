# EqualizerSoundEffect

**Superclass:** [SoundEffect](SoundEffect.md)

The `EqualizerSoundEffect` class controls the volume of audio across three frequency ranges (Low, Mid, and High) to selectively highlight or eliminate specific audio elements.

## Properties
### `EqualizerSoundEffect.HighGain`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of frequencies greater than 4000 Hz.

### `EqualizerSoundEffect.LowGain`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of frequencies lower than 400 Hz.

### `EqualizerSoundEffect.MidGain`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of frequencies between 400 and 4000 Hz.
