# NoCollisionConstraint

**Superclass:** [Instance](Instance.md)

NoCollisionConstraint is a constraint that prevents collisions between two specific parts, allowing the connection of other parts to those parts.

## Properties
### `NoCollisionConstraint.Enabled`
- **Type:** `boolean`
- **Summary:** Determines whether the two linked `Class.BasePart|BaseParts` will collide with each other.

### `NoCollisionConstraint.Part0`
- **Type:** `[BasePart](BasePart.md)`
- **Summary:** The second `Class.BasePart` that the constraint connects.

### `NoCollisionConstraint.Part1`
- **Type:** `[BasePart](BasePart.md)`
- **Summary:** The first `Class.BasePart` that the constraint connects.
