# AngularVelocity

**Superclass:** [Constraint](Constraint.md)

Applies torque to an assembly to maintain a constant angular velocity.

## Properties
### `AngularVelocity.AngularVelocity`
- **Type:** `Vector3`
- **Summary:** A `Datatype.Vector3` that gives the desired or target angular velocity.

### `AngularVelocity.MaxTorque`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Magnitude of the maximum torque the constraint can apply.

### `AngularVelocity.ReactionTorqueEnabled`
- **Type:** `boolean`
- **Summary:** Causes the constraint to apply equal and opposite reaction forces.

### `AngularVelocity.RelativeTo`
- **Type:** `ActuatorRelativeTo`
