# SelectionLasso

**Superclass:** [GuiBase3d](GuiBase3d.md)

SelectionLasso is an abstract class for objects that can be attached to `Class.Humanoid` or `Class.BasePart`, and can also be attached to a 3D space point.

## Tags
- NotCreatable

## Properties
### `SelectionLasso.Humanoid`
- **Type:** `[Humanoid](Humanoid.md)`
- **Summary:** The Humanoid that the Lasso belongs to, and will come from.
