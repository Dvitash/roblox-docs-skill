# BloomEffect

**Superclass:** [PostEffect](PostEffect.md)

BloomEffect is a post-processing effect that simulates the camera viewing a very bright light, causing brighter colors to glow. It can be applied to multiple objects and requires specific Studio settings for proper rendering.

## Properties
### `BloomEffect.Intensity`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the additive blending intensity.

### `BloomEffect.Size`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the radius of the bloom in pixels.

### `BloomEffect.Threshold`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines how bright a color must be before it blooms.
