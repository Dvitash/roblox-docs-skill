# AdGui

**Superclass:** [SurfaceGuiBase](SurfaceGuiBase.md)

AdGui is a class representing an AdGui element, which can include video ads and fallback images. It includes methods for reacting to events and has specific properties related to its content and behavior.

## Properties
### `AdGui.AdShape`
- **Type:** `AdShape`

### `AdGui.EnableVideoAds`
- **Type:** `boolean`
- **Summary:** Enables the AdGui to serve video ads.

### `AdGui.FallbackImage`
- **Type:** `ContentId`

### `AdGui.Status`
- **Type:** `AdUnitStatus`
- **Tags:** ReadOnly, NotReplicated
