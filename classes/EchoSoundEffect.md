# EchoSoundEffect

**Superclass:** [SoundEffect](SoundEffect.md)

EchoSoundEffect is a class for adding delayed repetitions of sounds with diminishing volume, which can be applied to individual sounds or sound groups.

## Properties
### `EchoSoundEffect.Delay`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The amount of time between echoes.

### `EchoSoundEffect.DryLevel`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of the original sound.

### `EchoSoundEffect.Feedback`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The echo decay every time the echo plays.

### `EchoSoundEffect.WetLevel`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The output volume of the echoed effect.
