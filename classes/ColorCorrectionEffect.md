# ColorCorrectionEffect

**Superclass:** [PostEffect](PostEffect.md)

ColorCorrectionEffect is a class that allows adjusting various color properties like saturation, tint, brightness, and contrast across the rendered world.

## Properties
### `ColorCorrectionEffect.Brightness`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines by how much the brightness of pixel colors will be shifted.

### `ColorCorrectionEffect.Contrast`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the change in separation between the dark and light colors.

### `ColorCorrectionEffect.Saturation`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** Determines the change in intensity of colors.

### `ColorCorrectionEffect.TintColor`
- **Type:** `Color3`
- **Summary:** Determines by how much the RGB channels of pixels are scaled.
