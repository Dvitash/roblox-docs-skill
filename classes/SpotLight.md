# SpotLight

**Superclass:** [Light](Light.md)

Spotlight is a light source that emits light in a cone shape, ideal for directional lighting like flashlights. It inherits from `Class.BasePart` and uses specific properties to determine the direction of light emission.

## Properties
### `SpotLight.Angle`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The angle of which the light is shone from the SpotLight.

### `SpotLight.Face`
- **Type:** `NormalId`
- **Summary:** Sets the side of the parent that the SpotLight comes from.

### `SpotLight.Range`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Summary:** The size of the area that the SpotLight will illuminate.
