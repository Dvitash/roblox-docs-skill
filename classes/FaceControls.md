# FaceControls

**Superclass:** [Instance](Instance.md)

The `Class.FaceControls` object defines properties for controlling the facial expressions of a Dynamic Head, which are based on Facial Action Coding System (FACS).

## Properties
### `FaceControls.ChinRaiser`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated
- **Summary:** Raises the chin up; moves the lower lip upwards.

### `FaceControls.ChinRaiserUpperLip`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated
- **Summary:** Moves the upper lip when ChinRaiser is engaged and touching the upper lip.

### `FaceControls.Corrugator`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated
- **Summary:** Brings the left and right brows inward together.

### `FaceControls.EyesLookDown`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated
- **Summary:** Moves gaze down. This is a required pose for avatars.

### `FaceControls.EyesLookLeft`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated

### `FaceControls.EyesLookRight`

### `FaceControls.EyesLookUp`

### `FaceControls.FlatPucker`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated
- **Summary:** Also known as lip tightener; brings the corners of the mouth inward and pressing the lips back against the teeth.

### `FaceControls.Funneler`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated
- **Summary:** Makes a 'O' shape with the mouth.

### `FaceControls.JawDrop`
- **Type:** `float`
- **Tags:** NotReplicated

### `FaceControls.JawLeft`

### `FaceControls.JawRight`
