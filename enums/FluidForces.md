# Enum.FluidForces

Controls the enablement of aerodynamic forces on parts and assemblies in the workspace.

## Items

| Name | Value | Tags | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Default` | 0 |  | Aerodynamic forces will not be calculated on any `Class.BasePart\|BaseParts`. |
| `Experimental` | 1 |  | Aerodynamic forces will be calculated on `Class.BasePart\|BaseParts` with `Class.BasePart.EnableFluidForces\|EnableFluidForces` set to true. During the beta phase, the behavior of the aerodynamic force model may change as the model improves. |

**Valid values:** `Enum.FluidForces.Default`, `Enum.FluidForces.Experimental`
