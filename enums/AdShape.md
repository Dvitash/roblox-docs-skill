# Enum.AdShape

Describes the form factor of an ad in an `Class.AdGui`. There is currently only one available size.

## Items

| Name | Value | Tags | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `HorizontalRectangle` | 1 |  | The ad has an aspect ratio of 16:9 and a minimum size of 8 by 4.5 studs. |

**Valid values:** `Enum.AdShape.HorizontalRectangle`
