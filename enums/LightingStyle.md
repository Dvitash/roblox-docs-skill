# Enum.LightingStyle

Enum used by `Class.Lighting.LightingStyle` to indicate the artistic intent behind lighting in the experience.

## Items

| Name | Value | Tags | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Realistic` | 0 |  | The most advanced and realistic lighting and shadows Roblox can deliver. |
| `Soft` | 1 |  | A flat, retro-Roblox look with softer lights and shadows. |

**Valid values:** `Enum.LightingStyle.Realistic`, `Enum.LightingStyle.Soft`
