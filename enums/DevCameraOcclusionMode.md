# Enum.DevCameraOcclusionMode

Determines how the default camera handles objects that are in-between the camera and the camera subject.

## Items

| Name | Value | Tags | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Zoom` | 0 |  | The camera zooms in until there is nothing between it and its subject. |
| `Invisicam` | 1 |  | Any objects between the camera and its subject become translucent locally. |

**Valid values:** `Enum.DevCameraOcclusionMode.Zoom`, `Enum.DevCameraOcclusionMode.Invisicam`
